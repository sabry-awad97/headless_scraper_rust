FAQ

What are the instructions?

First, plug product into a power outlet, then hold down the power switch for 2-3 seconds until the light turns on. You can adjust the temperature with the left and right buttons, the maximum temperature is 210° C degrees. 180° C degrees is the recommended ideal temperature.

Make sure your hair is dry, and then use the appliance to curl sections of your hair, starting from the top working your way down.

Once you have completed your full head of hair, make sure to hold the power button down until the device is showing as OFF, then allow it to cool normally, and store.

Does it work on all hair types?

The Tidal Hair Waver is suitable for hair of any thickness. For thicker hair you may want to use the styling tool on a higher heat setting, for thinner hair you may want to use on a lower heat setting. You can easily set and adjust the temperature yourself on the styling tool.

We've had a lot of feedback to suggest the waves can generally last for days! But it will be dependant on your hair type, how your hair holds heat, and the environment you are in e.g. dry vs humid.

Are the barrels Titanium or Ceramic?

The Tidal Hair Waver has ceramic barrels. Ceramic is great for smoothing out hair and reducing frizz, as well as holds heat very well and encourages even heat distribution. 

Is it easy to use?

Because our curler has 3 large barrels, it is extremely easy and quick to use. Simply clamp down, release and move onto the next part of your hair. You'll finish your full head of hair in no time.

If you can't get the hang of things, we offer 14 day returns - so make sure to get in touch with us within 14 days of recieving your product to issue a return.
